About us

About GIVE NGO

Get to know about our organization

GIVE NGO is a not-for-profit organization committed to creating positive change in the lives of underprivileged communities across the globe. Founded in 2017, by a group of passionate individuals, we believe that every person has the right to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive.


Our mission is to empower vulnerable individuals and communities, ensuring that they have the tools and resources needed to uplift themselves from challenging circumstances. At GIVE NGO, we are dedicated to fostering an environment where dreams are realized, potentials are unlocked, and hope is renewed.
