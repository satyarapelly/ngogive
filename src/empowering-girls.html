Empowering Girls - One Pad At a Time

Sthree Swabhiman is a large-scale menstrual hygiene and dignity initiative implemented by Give For Society in collaboration with the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Telangana Backward Classes Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society (MJPTBCWREIS) across Telangana. The program supports adolescent girls in residential schools, junior colleges, and degree colleges by promoting menstrual health, hygiene, and dignity through structured awareness sessions and educational outreach. By fostering understanding, confidence, and hygienic practices, the initiative helps girls manage menstruation safely and with self-assurance. This state-level effort reaches more than 1.23 lakh students from socially and economically disadvantaged communities, building awareness and dignity through education and empowerment.

Project Details

Sthree Swabhiman advances menstrual hygiene and dignity for adolescent girls in MJPTBCWREIS institutions. The program builds a supportive environment for girls to stay in school with confidence, while improving awareness of safe and responsible hygiene practices.

  • Promote menstrual health and hygiene through awareness and education.

  • Reduce stigma and absenteeism by normalizing open conversations.

  • Enable girls to manage their menstrual health with confidence and dignity.

Interactive menstrual hygiene sessions are conducted annually in each girls’ school and junior college using culturally sensitive Telugu-language flipbooks, posters, and leaflets. Each newly enrolled girl receives a one-time starter sanitary kit with simple guides on safe usage and disposal to encourage hygienic practices and self-care.
